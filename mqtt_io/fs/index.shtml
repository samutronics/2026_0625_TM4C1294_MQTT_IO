Relays are exposed as MQTT switches: send ON / OFF to <base>/relay/<n>/set and the state is retained on <base>/relay/<n>/state (also auto-discovered by Home Assistant). Inputs are read from the SN65HVS882 chain. On connect the device publishes a retained online to <base>/status; its last will is offline. Settings are stored in EEPROM and survive reset.
