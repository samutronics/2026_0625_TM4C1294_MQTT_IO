Outputs

Standard outputs switch ON/OFF and stay. Timed outputs turn OFF automatically after the Auto-off time; any ON restarts the timer, OFF cancels it. Pulse closes the relay for the given time regardless of mode. Assign each named output to a room on the Rooms tab’s dashboard via the Room column here.

Shutters

A shutter pairs two relays (Up/Down), interlocked so only one energizes at a time with a mandatory 500 ms delay on direction change. Each direction runs for the Travel time, then stops. The two relays are hidden from the Outputs list and exposed to Home Assistant as a cover.
Buttons: ▲ Up / ▼ Down are momentary — press while moving to stop; ■ Stop; ↻ is single-button cycle (up / stop / down). They act on the saved configuration — add or edit a shutter, then Save Configuration before using them.
Pushbuttons: in the Inputs table bind a pushbutton to this shutter's ▲Up or ▼Down entry with action Turn ON (momentary up/down — press again while moving to stop), Turn OFF (stop), or Toggle (single-button up/stop/down cycle).

Rooms

Name the rooms/zones you want on the Control dashboard, then assign each light (Room column on the Outputs tab) and shutter (Room column on the Shutters tab) to a room. Leave a room name blank to remove it. Unassigned actuators appear under “Unassigned”.
