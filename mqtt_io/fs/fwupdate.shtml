Firmware Update (OTA)

0%
How it works: The binary is uploaded in chunks to on-chip staging flash, then verified by CRC32. The device resets and applies the update automatically.
Generate the binary in CCS: Project → Properties → Build → Steps → Post-build: tiarmclang --tool objcopy -O binary mqtt_io.out mqtt_io.bin
Do not navigate away during upload. The device will reboot; reconnect after ~10 seconds.
